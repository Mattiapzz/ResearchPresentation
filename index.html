Road Representation

  • Waylines: road cross section.
  • Waypoints: sampled in the waylines.
  • Topology aware meshing

Obstacle Handling

Obstacle Prediction

  • Urban: constant velocity model or minimum-time trajectory.
  • Additional waylines at intersection.
  • Waypoints in inhibited regions discarded.

Semi-structured RRT*

  • Anytime approach: finite-time solution.
  • Mixed sampling: Warm start, Structured and Random
  • Connections with optimal motion primitives.

Cost Function

Urban multi-objective + negotiation (Piazza et al. 2024, 2025)

Urban multi-objective

\[ J_{MO}={ \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{N_s}} \left(W_T~ J_T^i + W_L~ J_L^i + W_V~ J_{v_{x}}^i + W_{j_x}~ J_{j_x}^i + W_{a_y}~ J_{a_y}^i\right) \]

+ negotiation

\[ \begin{aligned} J=&J_{MO} + {\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{N_s}} \left({ W_{\mathrm{Neg}}~ \sum_{i=0}^N\int_{0}^{T}\Gamma_{\mathrm{des}}(d_{\mathrm{des}_i})\mathrm{d}t} \right) \\ \Gamma_{\mathrm{des}}(d_{\mathrm{des}_i})=& \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} 1 & d_{\mathrm{des}_i} \le 0\\ 1 - \frac{d_{\mathrm{des}_i}}{d_{\mathrm{des_{\mathrm{safe}}}}} & 0<d_{\mathrm{des}_i}<d_{\mathrm{des_{\mathrm{safe}}}}\\ 0 & d_{\mathrm{des}_i} \ge d_{\mathrm{des_{\mathrm{safe}}}} \end{array} \right. \end{aligned} \]

Cost Function

Racing




Minimum Travel Time + Penalties

\[ J_{MO}={ \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{N_s}} W_T~ J_T^i \]

Where \(J_T^i\) depends only on the travel time.

Of course, we can integrate other cost functions to improve safety, robustness, and continuity with the previous solution.

Performance and limits

Performance

  • Asimptotic optimality.
  • Complex manouvres combinations.
  • Feasible solution for the vehicle model.
  • \(\sim600\) MP in \(50\,ms\) with 2-3 obstacles (C++ on Intel i5 @ 4.5 GHz).

Limits

  • Computational time could be enhanced.
  • Quality of the solution highly depends on the harware.
    • \(\sim200\) MP on dSPace Autera (Intel Xeon 12 cores @ 2.0 GHz)
  • Time lost to evaluate useless solutions
    • Manouvre cost is evaluated up to the node
    • No matrics to evalaute solution forward.

Problem statement

OCP

\[ \begin{aligned} \min \; T & = \min \int_0^L \frac{w_T}{\dot{s}} \, \mathrm{d}\zeta \\ \mathrm{s.t.} \quad &\mathbf{A}(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{u}) \, \mathbf{\dot{x}} = \mathbf{b}(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{u}) \\ \mathrm{Cyclic} \; \mathrm{b.c.} \quad &\mathbf{x}(0) = \mathbf{x}(L)\\ % &\dot{s}(\zeta) = \frac{u(\zeta) \cos(\xi(\zeta)) - v(\zeta) \sin(\xi(\zeta))}{1-\kappa(\zeta) n(\zeta)} ) \end{aligned} \]

Vehicle Dynamics

  • Rigid body model, internal DoF, slip dynamics (\(30\) states).
  • Engine torque envelope + chain tensioning effect.
  • Soft penetration (tyre ground).
  • Full tyre model (MF-MCTyre 1.1).
  • Aerodynamic maps for drag lift and application point.

(suspensions, steer, rider body position, wheels).

MLT for MotoGP

How to initialize the problem

Does everything work right out of the box? No, for sure this is a perfect recipe for a disaster. So, Idea… Solve a similar problem more treatable (less complex), convex, and quadratic. Then solve iteratively a problem converging to the original one.

Initialization

  • Convex and quadratic problem
  • Simple and fast solution
  • Iterative solution
  • Convergence to the original problem
  • Warm start for the original problem

\[ \min \int_0^L \frac{w_T}{\dot{s}} \, \mathrm{d}\zeta \]

Tranformed into a “more” quadratic problem

Other terms might be added

\[ \min \int_0^L \left( \frac{w_T}{\dot{s}} + (\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{x_{qss}})^T \mathbf{W_{qss}} (\mathbf{x}-\mathbf{x_{qss}}) \right) \, \mathrm{d}\zeta \]

MLT for MotoGP

Continuation - Homotopy

  1. \(w_T << 1\), \(\mathbf{W_{qss}} = 1\) (Constant velocity). Fixed initial conditions and free final conditions
  2. \(w_T = 1\), \(\mathbf{W_{qss}} = 0\) (Optimal time). Fixed initial conditions and free final conditions
  3. Fixed initial conditions and final conditions equal to the initial conditions
  4. Cyclic conditions
  5. Push the penalty values on constraints
